First, I started by experimenting with just one Neopixel. Below is how the circuit was first hooked up.

Then, I hooked up two Neopixels to a breadboard. This was a little more difficult than hooking up just one Neopixel, however, I did not have too much trouble with it.

EXPERIMENTATION - Light

With this circuit running, I decided I would play around with different light effects by finding household items to diffuse or reflect the light from the Neopixels.

Agate Rock

This piece was something I could pass the chance to try. Thankfully I did, because the results are beautiful! The two coloured lights really brought out the detail in the piece of crystal and gave it a very pretty colour.

Plastic Bag

I really like the way the light reflects on the plastic, as it gives the bag a "fractured" feel.

Glue Stick

This one was a bit of a surprise. I was not expecting the whole stick to glow like it did.

Foil

Finally, this was my favorite experiment. I could not believe the kind of beautiful images I was able to get from just a piece of foil and some lights. The reflected light feels almost like electricity running through the foil, which I think is really cool.SS
